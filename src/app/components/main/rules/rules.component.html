Rules

 

** RUMMY PLAY 2 CASH **

 

  1] Rummy card game is played between 2 to 6 players with two decks of cards.

  2] The player has to draw and discard cards to form valid sets and sequences of the 13 cards in hand.

  3] To start, each player have 13 cards. One random card is picked as the Wild Card or Joker card of the game. To create impure sequence and sets using wild or joker card.

  4] Once all the 13 cards are arranged in valid sequences and groups, he can make a declaration and win the game.

  5] In rummy, a sequence is a group of three or more consecutive cards of the same suit.

  6] A pure sequence is a group of three or more cards of the same suit, placed in consecutive order. A player cannot use any Joker or wild card.

  7] An impure sequence is a group of three or more cards of the same suit with one or more Joker card used. A set is group of three or four cards of the same value but  of different suits.

   8] SET 1 compulsory & SET 2 if needed to complete 13 Cards in sets and sequences. Made with 3 or 4 Cards only. Cards of same value & different suits, can you use joker or wild card.
